Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a heat pump cost after the grant?

After the ¬£7,500 grant, most homeowners pay between ¬£3,500-¬£5,500 for a complete air source heat pump installation, depending on property size and system requirements.

How long does the installation take?

A typical heat pump installation takes 2-3 days. The installer will handle all the paperwork for your grant application.

Do I need to improve my insulation first?

As of 2025, you no longer need to complete insulation improvements before claiming the grant, though good insulation helps your heat pump work more efficiently.

Can I get a heat pump if I have radiators?

Yes! Most modern heat pumps work with existing radiators. In some cases, a few radiators might need upgrading for optimal efficiency.

How much can I save on my bills?

Typical savings range from ¬£200-¬£500 per year depending on your current heating system, property size, and energy tariff. Heat pumps are 300% efficient compared to gas boilers at 90%.

Are heat pumps noisy?

Modern heat pumps are very quiet - typically around 40-60 decibels, similar to a modern fridge. They're quieter than most conversations.
